Hi, I'm Lucia. I am a game programmer who is a generalist in the sense that I love pretty much every area of programming, so it is hard for me to choose a specialization. Taking on new challenges is always a personal goal for me on every project that I am a part of. I am very passionate about learning and trying new techniques to grow my abilities as a programmer.

I have experience working on both solo and collaborative projects. I am a strong team player, and I have received lots of positive feedback, both for my contributions and my critique.

I have the most experience in Unity / C#, but I am also well acquainted with Unreal and C++.

I am very passionate about making games, especially ones with creative gameplay design and ones that intend to have an emotional impact on its players. Even though programming is my focus, I greatly enjoy contributing to design conversations as well.

When I am not programming, I like to cross-stitch, make pixel art, and play board games and videogames. My favorite genres are probably roguelites and platformers, but I have games that I love in almost every genre I can think of.
